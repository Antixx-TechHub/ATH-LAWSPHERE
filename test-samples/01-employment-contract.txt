RECITALS:
WHEREAS, the Company desires to employ the Employee as a Senior Software Engineer; and
WHEREAS, the Employee desires to be employed by the Company on the terms and conditions set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1 - EMPLOYMENT AND DUTIES

1.1 Position: The Employee shall serve as Senior Software Engineer reporting to the Engineering Manager.

1.2 Duties: The Employee shall perform duties including but not limited to:
    (a) Design and development of software applications
    (b) Code review and quality assurance
    (c) Mentoring junior team members
    (d) Participation in sprint planning and agile ceremonies

1.3 Best Efforts: The Employee agrees to devote their full professional time, attention, and best efforts to the performance of their duties.

ARTICLE 2 - TERM AND TERMINATION

2.1 Commencement Date: The employment shall commence on February 1, 2025.

2.2 At-Will Employment: This employment is "at-will" subject to the notice period specified herein.

2.3 Notice Period: Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing:
    (a) During probation (first 6 months): 15 days written notice
    (b) After probation: 60 days written notice

2.4 Termination for Cause: The Company may terminate employment immediately without notice for:
    (a) Gross misconduct or insubordination
    (b) Material breach of this Agreement
    (c) Conviction of any criminal offense
    (d) Fraudulent conduct or dishonesty
    (e) Violation of Company policies

ARTICLE 3 - COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS

3.1 Base Salary: The Employee shall receive an annual CTC of INR 24,00,000 (Indian Rupees Twenty-Four Lakhs Only), payable monthly, subject to applicable tax deductions.

3.2 Breakdown:
    - Basic Salary: INR 10,00,000 per annum
    - HRA: INR 4,00,000 per annum
    - Special Allowance: INR 6,00,000 per annum
    - PF Contribution (Employer): INR 1,20,000 per annum
    - Gratuity: INR 48,000 per annum
    - Medical Insurance Premium: INR 32,000 per annum
    - Flexible Benefits: INR 2,00,000 per annum

3.3 Performance Bonus: Subject to performance review, the Employee may be eligible for an annual bonus of up to 15% of the annual CTC.

3.4 Stock Options: The Employee shall be eligible for the Company's ESOP scheme after completing 1 year of service.

ARTICLE 4 - LEAVE POLICY

4.1 Annual Leave: 18 days paid leave per calendar year (prorated for partial years)
4.2 Sick Leave: 12 days per calendar year (non-encashable)
4.3 Public Holidays: As per the Company's holiday calendar
4.4 Maternity/Paternity Leave: As per applicable law

ARTICLE 5 - CONFIDENTIALITY

5.1 Confidential Information: The Employee agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information, trade secrets, business strategies, client lists, technical data, and any other confidential information belonging to the Company.

5.2 Duration: This confidentiality obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement for a period of 3 years.

5.3 Return of Materials: Upon termination, the Employee shall return all Company property, documents, and materials.

ARTICLE 6 - INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

6.1 Work Product: All inventions, discoveries, designs, software, documents, and other work product created by the Employee during employment shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

6.2 Assignment: The Employee hereby assigns all rights, title, and interest in such work product to the Company.

6.3 Moral Rights: To the extent permitted by law, the Employee waives any moral rights in the work product.

ARTICLE 7 - NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

7.1 Non-Compete: During employment and for 12 months thereafter, the Employee shall not engage in any business that directly competes with the Company within Maharashtra.

7.2 Non-Solicitation: For 12 months after termination, the Employee shall not:
    (a) Solicit any employees of the Company
    (b) Solicit any clients or customers of the Company

ARTICLE 8 - DISPUTE RESOLUTION

8.1 Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

8.2 Arbitration: Any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall be resolved by arbitration in Pune under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

8.3 Jurisdiction: The courts of Pune shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

ARTICLE 9 - GENERAL PROVISIONS

9.1 Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties.

9.2 Amendment: This Agreement may only be amended in writing signed by both parties.

9.3 Severability: If any provision is found invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in effect.

9.4 Waiver: Failure to enforce any provision shall not constitute a waiver.
